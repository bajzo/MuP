Raj Marwah

Founder CEO

Vienna, Austria

For the last 21 years, Raj has executed major mergers and acquisitions in Europe and Asia on behalf of multinational clients based out of Hong Kong, Germany, and the USA. Raj also has more than 21 years of worldwide advertising and marketing experience, working globally for multinational accounts in Sydney, Hong Kong, Toronto, Auckland, Dubai, Vienna, Bangkok, and New York.

Michael Klinger

Periodic Private Equity partner

Vienna, Austria

Austrian-born Michael Klinger is an experienced senior manager having served as CFO for several US corporations. Michael served as the Financial Officer on the Management Board of a large Gas Distribution Company in Central Europe, and has also been the Regional (Europe) Vice President for Western Union International.

Anthony Ainsworth

Advisory

Southeast Asia

Sir Anthony has worked in Asia since 1993 in Hong Kong, Hanoi, and Phnom Penh before settling in Bangkok in 1999. Sir Anthony has dual English and Irish nationality. Anthony was educated at Harrow School and Sandhurst Royal Military Academy. He served as an officer with the British Army in Germany.

Donald Lemon

International finance

Dallas, Texas

Donald Lemon has spent most of his 35-year professional career (MUP associate since 2000) in financial management and legal services based in the US, China, Hong Kong, and Southeast Asia. A licensed US lawyer, Don is also fluent in Vietnamese, a country where he worked for more than a decade.

Erkki Toljamo

Enterpreneur

Helsinki, Finland

Erkki Toljamo is a Finnish professional with extensive contacts and relationships in the region. He has proven expertise in identifying and appraising investment opportunities, setting up teams and closing investment rounds. He has worked on various deals with MUP for over 15 years.

Martin Maltais

Entrepreuneur, educator

Quebec, Canada

Prof. Dr. Martin Maltais is a university professor on finance and educational policy at Universite du Quebec a Rimouski (UQAR), and head of the Department of Education. Prof. Dr. Maltais is a member of the Scientific Committee of the Optics National Institute (INO), a non-profit organization that incubated 32 business successes over the last 30 years in optics and phonics. He is a former advisor to the Minister of Higher Education, Quebec (until 7/2020).

Stefan Stoiber

Lawyer

Vienna, Austria

Dr. Stefan Stoiber, is a leading Austrian attorney and expert in international business. Dr. Stoiber formed a Vienna law practice in 1989 working mainly in the field of civil law, representing small and medium sized enterprises, in particular Austrian branches and subsidiaries of foreign groups in all legal aspects. A large number of foreign clients are served primarily in English, but the law firm also works with Chinese companies and corresponds in Mandarin.

T.P. Sreenivasan

Former Ambassador of India

Kerala, India

Former Ambassador of India and Governor for India of the IAEA. Ambassador Sreenivasan is the Executive Vice Chancellor of the Kerala State Higher Education Council, and is the Director General of the Kerala International Centre. The Council has the mandate to advise the Government of Kerala on all matters pertaining to the universities and other institutions of higher learning in Kerala.
